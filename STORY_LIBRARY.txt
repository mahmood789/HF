## Act I · Arrival & Rising Water
----------------------------------------------------------------

### Scene 1 – Rain Gathers
*Sam (diary)* Rain scribbles diagonal lines on the ward window, erasing parked-car halos. Numbers on Amina’s chart blur—BNP 218 tries to read 812; I blink and win. She knits a navy scarf, pauses to rub her ankles where crescent oedema tugs at skin. We talk of Yusuf’s geometry test; the word “angle” tilts inside my head. I raise her bed; jugular tide rises, falls. My pen slows to the rain’s beat.  
*Amina (diary)* Needles click, rain replies. Dr Sam studies my neck more than the scarf. I joke about Yusuf’s missing protractor; he laughs but never stops counting beats.  
**🧠 Question** Which sign favours heart failure over COPD?  
- A. Raised JVP  
- B. Long wheeze  
- C. Crackles  
**✅ Correct** A **💡 Hint** Neck rarely lies. **📘 Pearl** JVP > 4 cm strongly suggests HF. **🏅 XP** diagnostic
